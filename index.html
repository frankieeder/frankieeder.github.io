why; are you paying for this?

(three-channel video installation)

cw: sexual violence, flashing lights
Isolating and algorithmically affecting scenes from cinema that romanticize sexual manipulation, this piece offers alternate, synchronized views that question physical and emotional autonomy of survivors in the digital age of hollywood.

Channel 1 (left): Unmodified, theatrical excerpts
Channel 2 (middle): Heavily modified excerpt that cycles at a high human heart rate.
Channel 3 (right): Degraded view of theatrical excerpts, physically opposing Channel 1.

Concatenations II

(photo collection)

Created by algorithmically combining, filtering, and disfiguring sequenced images of LGBTQ+ individuals, Concatenations II investigates and deconstructs ideas of queer visual representation, recognition, censorship, and identity in a modern-day technology-driven socioeconomic landscape.
